## Brand & Visual System Spec
The design system is engineered for a premium, clean, light-mode CRM experience that reduces visual clutter. 

The brand personality is **refreshing, organic, and premium**, utilizing soft **Ocean Teal (#0D9488)** as the main color, **Mint Ice (#F0FDFA)** as the background gradient floor, and warm **Champagne Gold (#D97706)** as the accent and highlight color.

## Layout & Components
- **Kanban Column Headers:** Stylized with capsule-pill shapes in soft teal gradients, with Outfit typography.
- **Lead Cards:** Pure Alabaster White (#FFFFFF) with rounded-2xl (1.5rem) corners, thin outline borders, and soft ambient shadows.
- **Sidebars:** Translucent glassmorphic dark-teal gradient sidebars.
